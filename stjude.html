Dear Brian,

Amid the hopeful signs this spring offers, we cherish the chance to pause and celebrate graduations for St. Jude kids.

As the little ones delight in completing kindergarten and the teens mark the rite of passage from high school, we are mindful of people like you.

When you support St. Jude, you help make possible the bright futures symbolized by these milestone events.

After a devastating diagnosis, after many dark days enduring awful diseases, these remarkable kids can look forward to a second chance at life.

As parents contemplate the possibilities for their kids, they feel immense gratitude for generous people dedicated to furthering the St. Jude mission.

Because supporters ensure families never receive a bill from St. Jude, moms and dads can focus on helping their child live to experience moments like these.

We are more determined than ever to give them hope they will see their child grow up, even when faced with a dire diagnosis. It is why St. Jude recently announced a bold plan to invest in a historic expansion of our work to accelerate research and treatment for kids everywhere.

We are once again thankful to you for supporting the kids and families of St. Jude — we won’t stop until no child dies of cancer.

Warmest regards,

Richard C. Shadyac Jr.
President & CEO
ALSAC, the fundraising and awareness organization
for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
